# Cash — Personal Finance & Loan Manager (EN)

**Subtitle (max 30 chars):** Private finance & budget app

**Promo text (max 170 chars):** Track income and expenses, import OFX and manage loans. Use budgets and advanced reports. Optional iCloud sync and privacy-first data handling.
## Full Description
Cash helps you take control of your personal finances with a clean interface and powerful features:

- Track income, expenses and transfers quickly and easily
- Import transactions with OFX files for a smooth migration from banks and other tools
- Powerful budgeting with envelopes to plan and control monthly spending
- Manage loans, mortgages and amortization schedules; calculate payoffs and compare repayment scenarios
- Create recurring transactions and reconcile your accounts
- Advanced reports: net worth, forward projections, expense-by-category, year-over-year and long-term forecasts
- Optional iCloud sync to keep your data across devices; all data is stored locally by default
- Privacy-first: we never access your data in iCloud — your data stays yours

Cash offers a freemium model. Premium unlocks iCloud sync, advanced reports, budgets and loan features via subscription. Try the free version to see if Cash fits your needs.
