# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 5, 2025

## Overview

Cash is a personal finance management application designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

**Cash does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** All your financial information stays on your device.

### What data is stored locally:
- Account information (names, types, balances)
- Transaction records
- Budget configurations
- App preferences and settings

### What data is NOT collected:
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Advertising identifiers

## iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:
- Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- Only you can access your data through your Apple ID
- We have no access to your iCloud data

## Third-Party Services

Cash does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- iCloud data (if enabled) is protected by Apple's security measures

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- Export your data at any time
- Delete all data through the app settings
- Disable iCloud sync to keep data local only

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on our project page.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please visit our project page or open an issue on GitHub.
